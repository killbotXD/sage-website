How to Use SAGE: From Patient Creation to Prescription

March 25, 2026 · 2 min read · Guide

SAGE is designed to get you from patient intake to a professional PDF prescription in under two minutes. Whether you're a solo practitioner or running a busy clinic, here's exactly how it works — step by step.

1

Select or Add a Patient

Start by searching for an existing patient or creating a new one. When adding a new patient, you'll enter their basic details:

  • Name, age, gender, and contact number
  • Vitals — blood pressure, weight, temperature, pulse
  • Medical history, allergies, and current medications
  • Family history and risk factors

For returning patients, SAGE pulls up their complete history instantly. No more flipping through paper files.

2

Record Symptoms and Diagnosis

Document the patient's presenting complaints and your clinical findings. SAGE provides smart auto-complete suggestions as you type, so common symptoms and diagnoses are just a few keystrokes away.

You can record multiple diagnoses per visit and add clinical notes for your own reference.

3

Add Medicines and Investigations

This is where SAGE really saves you time. Start typing a medicine name and the smart search pulls from a comprehensive Indian medicine database. For each medicine, you'll set:

  • Dosage (pre-filled options for common doses)
  • Frequency — OD, BD, TDS, QID, SOS, and more
  • Duration — quick presets for 3, 5, 7, 15, 30 days
  • Special instructions — before/after meals, with water, etc.

Need lab work? Add investigations like blood tests, X-rays, CT, or MRI right from the same screen. Mark urgent tests and include special instructions for the lab.

4

Provide Advice and Follow-up

Add patient advice — dietary recommendations, activity restrictions, warning signs to watch for, or lifestyle modifications. Set a follow-up date so both you and the patient have a clear plan.

This section appears on the final prescription, giving patients a clear takeaway beyond just their medicines.

5

Generate PDF Prescription

Hit the generate button and SAGE produces a clean, professional PDF prescription that includes:

  • Your clinic header and doctor details
  • Patient information and vitals
  • Diagnosis, medicines with dosage instructions
  • Investigations, advice, and follow-up date

The prescription is ready to print or share digitally. It's stored in the cloud automatically, so you can access it anytime from any device.

Pro tip: Use SAGE's template system to save your most common prescriptions. For repeat visits or similar cases, load a template and adjust — you'll go from patient to prescription in under 30 seconds.

SAGE supports multiple Indian languages, so your patients can read their prescription in Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, and more. Everything is backed up daily to the cloud with bank-level encryption.
